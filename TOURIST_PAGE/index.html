Top three activities to do in Vancouver

Stanley Park

A 405-hectare public park that borders downtown Vancouver. It's a must-see for its scenic views, wildlife, and outdoor activities.

Granville Island

A peninsula and shopping district in Vancouver. It's home to the Granville Island Public Market, which has a variety of food and artisan shops.

Capilano Suspension

A popular tourist attraction in North Vancouver. It features a suspension bridge, treetop adventures, and a cliffwalk.
